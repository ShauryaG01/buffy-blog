Editorial Policy

Our standards for evidence, independence, updates, and corrections.

BBuffy EditorialReviewed · 6 min read

Evidence standards

  • Pricing and plans are checked against official vendor pages or marketplace listings.
  • Product features are supported by official documentation.
  • Benchmarks link to the underlying study and retain its scope and date.
  • Material claims without adequate evidence are removed or the page remains excluded from search indexing.

How pages are produced

Automation may help organize drafts, but publication requires evidence checks, a page-quality review, and a successful technical validation. Sources and verification dates are stored with each page.

Conflicts of interest

Buffy operates an AI-commerce product and may appear in comparisons. We disclose that interest, describe material limitations, and do not sell rankings or placements.

Updates and corrections

Fast-changing commercial pages are rechecked regularly. When a material error is confirmed, we correct the page, update its review date, and reassess its index status.
