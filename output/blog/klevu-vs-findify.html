Klevu vs Findify: Shopify Search Compared (2026)

Klevu and Findify head-to-head - pricing, AI features, and which store each fits.

BBuffy Intel2026-07-09 · 5 min read

Overview

Klevu vs Findify: Shopify Search Compared (2026) comes up when a Shopify merchant has outgrown native search and is comparing two AI-driven platforms built for mid-market-to-enterprise stores, not startup budgets. Short verdict: Findify publishes its pricing outright - Premium $499/mo, Professional $799/mo, Enterprise $1199/mo - so a growth marketer can budget in minutes. Klevu, now part of Athos Commerce, only sells Onsite Discovery and Complete Discovery through custom quotes, aimed at mid-market and enterprise retailers with dedicated merchandising teams. If you want transparent, self-serve pricing plus personalized search and recommendations, start with Findify. If you need deep semantic search, self-learning ranking, and enterprise-grade merchandising and are comfortable negotiating a contract, talk to Klevu's sales team. Neither tool is built for a smaller DTC brand just trying to fix a weak on-site search box - for that stage, and for founders (including fast-scaling teams in markets like India) who want on-site AI search bundled with off-site AI-visibility, a leaner platform like Buffy is worth evaluating before committing several thousand dollars a year to either enterprise vendor.

Best For

Pick Findify if you want to see the price before talking to sales - Premium at $499/mo covers core personalized search, and Professional at $799/mo adds stronger merchandising controls as your catalog grows. Pick Klevu if you're an enterprise or mid-market Shopify Plus brand with a complex catalog and a merchandising team that needs custom-tuned semantic search and self-learning ranking - budget for a quote-based contract, not a monthly plan. Pick Findify's Enterprise tier ($1199/mo) if you've outgrown Professional but want to avoid the longer sales-negotiation cycle that comes with Klevu. Pick Buffy if you're a DTC brand that needs solid on-site AI search now but also cares about showing up in AI shopping assistants and answer engines - Klevu and Findify only address the on-site half of that problem.

Pros And Cons

Klevu - Pros: AI semantic search understands shopper intent beyond keyword matching; self-learning ranking improves relevance over time without constant manual tuning; merchandising toolset backed by Athos Commerce's enterprise focus. Cons: no public pricing means every evaluation starts with a sales call; positioning skews enterprise, so smaller catalogs may pay for capability they won't use; quoting and implementation timelines run longer than a self-serve tool.

Findify - Pros: transparent, tiered pricing (Premium $499/mo, Professional $799/mo, Enterprise $1199/mo) makes budgeting straightforward; personalized search, smart collections, and recommendations ship as one connected system; a free trial lets you validate fit before committing. Cons: $499/mo entry is a real hurdle for smaller stores; moving up to Professional or Enterprise for advanced merchandising adds up quickly; built for mid-market/enterprise scale, so smaller catalogs may not see proportional ROI.

Recommendation

If pricing transparency and speed matter, Findify wins - you can see all three tiers, start a free trial, and get personalized search live without a sales cycle. If you run an enterprise or complex mid-market Shopify Plus catalog and need the most sophisticated semantic search and merchandising, Klevu is the stronger long-term platform, but budget for a custom quote-based contract and a sales-led rollout. Neither tool is priced or positioned for an early-stage DTC brand - Findify's cheapest plan alone is $499/mo before merchandising add-ons, and Klevu doesn't publish pricing at all. If your store isn't yet at the scale that justifies either, or if AI visibility beyond your own site matters as much as on-site search, evaluate Buffy alongside these two before signing an annual enterprise contract.

Faq

How much does Klevu cost compared to Findify?

Findify publishes pricing: Premium at $499/mo, Professional at $799/mo, and Enterprise at $1199/mo. Klevu doesn't publish pricing - since merging into Athos Commerce, both its Onsite Discovery and Complete Discovery plans are quote-based, so you'll need to talk to sales to get a number, and it's positioned for mid-market/enterprise budgets rather than a self-serve monthly fee.

Is Findify worth $499/mo for a Shopify store?

It depends on catalog size and whether personalized search, smart collections, and recommendations as a bundle actually move conversion for you. For larger Shopify brands with enough traffic and SKUs, Findify's Premium tier can pay for itself; for a smaller catalog, $499/mo is a lot to commit before you've validated the lift, so use the free trial to check results before moving up to Professional or Enterprise.

Which is easier to set up: Klevu or Findify?

Findify is the easier of the two to get running on Shopify - it has published pricing, a free trial, and self-serve onboarding aimed at mid-market brands. Klevu's process starts with sales and quoting before implementation even begins, which adds time upfront - a natural fit if you already have an enterprise rollout team, less so if you want to move fast.

Does Klevu or Findify have better AI search?

Both are genuinely AI-driven rather than keyword search with a new label. Klevu's strength is semantic search and self-learning ranking tuned for enterprise merchandising; Findify's strength is personalization, with search, collections, and recommendations working off the same customer signal. Which is 'better' depends on whether your priority is deep merchandising control (Klevu) or bundled personalization (Findify) - neither publishes benchmark data for a direct head-to-head comparison.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Klevu and Findify?

Yes - both are built for mid-market and enterprise Shopify stores, so if you're earlier-stage, it's worth looking at leaner Shopify search apps or a platform like Buffy that pairs on-site AI search with AI-visibility work, rather than paying enterprise rates for capability you won't fully use yet. Verify current pricing on any alternative before comparing it against Findify's published tiers or a Klevu quote.
